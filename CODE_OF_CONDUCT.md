# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standards

Contributors must communicate respectfully, give actionable technical feedback,
and avoid harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and disclosure of
private information.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately through the repository maintainers or
GitHub's reporting tools. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may
temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.

This policy applies in repository discussions, reviews, issues, and other
AdrenAI community spaces.
