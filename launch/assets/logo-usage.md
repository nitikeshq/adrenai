# Logo And Thumbnail Usage

This document defines temporary launch rules until a formal AdrenAI brand
system is approved. Do not invent additional logo variants during production.

## Required Assets

Produce these assets before launch:

| Asset | Format | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Primary wordmark | SVG | Gallery slides and documentation |
| Primary symbol | SVG | Product Hunt thumbnail and social avatar |
| Light wordmark | SVG | Dark backgrounds |
| Dark wordmark | SVG | Light backgrounds |
| Square thumbnail | PNG, 1:1 | Product Hunt thumbnail |
| Social preview | PNG, 1200 x 630 | Link sharing |

Keep editable source files outside the upload exports. Product Hunt gallery
slides must use rasterized exports.

## Logo Rules

- Use the official AdrenAI spelling and capitalization.
- Keep the symbol and wordmark proportions unchanged.
- Maintain clear space equal to at least the height of the capital `A`.
- Place the logo on a quiet, high-contrast background.
- Use one logo treatment per asset.
- Keep the wordmark secondary to the slide headline.
- Use the symbol alone only where the full wordmark would be unreadable.

## Do Not

- Stretch, rotate, skew, outline, or add shadows to the logo.
- Place the logo on visually noisy terminal output.
- Recolor individual parts without an approved brand token.
- Add "AI" sparkles, gradients, or animation unless they are part of the
  approved logo source.
- Combine AdrenAI's logo with agent-provider logos into one mark.
- Imply endorsement or partnership with Codex, Claude Code, Copilot, Cursor,
  Kiro, or Gemini.

## Product Hunt Thumbnail

- Use a square canvas, preferably at least `512 x 512 px`.
- Center the AdrenAI symbol with generous clear space.
- Use a single high-contrast background.
- Avoid text beyond the symbol; it will be unreadable at small sizes.
- Preview at `64 x 64 px` and `32 x 32 px`.
- Export PNG and verify that the final file remains crisp.

## Provider Logo Usage

Provider logos may appear only as small supporting identifiers in diagrams.
Before publishing:

- verify each provider's current trademark and logo guidelines;
- use official assets without modification;
- include provider names when a logo may be ambiguous;
- avoid visual treatment suggesting an official partnership; and
- remove any provider logo whose usage rights are unclear.

When uncertain, use plain-text agent names instead of logos.

## Accessibility Checks

- Verify logo-to-background contrast in light and dark treatments.
- Ensure important meaning is not communicated by color alone.
- Add useful alt text when assets are used outside Product Hunt.
- Confirm that the square symbol remains recognizable at `32 x 32 px`.
