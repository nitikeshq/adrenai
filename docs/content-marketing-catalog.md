# SEO, Content, Writing, and Email Catalog

The `content` taxonomy provides reviewed starting strategies for technical SEO,
content SEO, long-form writing, and permission-based email marketing. Each
category contains at least 20 independently selectable strategies.

The reference workflows require explicit approvals and evidence for research,
outlining, drafting, review, delivery, compliance, and measurement. They do not
execute publishing or email delivery. Offline Markdown briefs allow the same
planning process without an AI provider.

Catalog descriptions are original AdrenAI guidance. Users remain responsible
for validating claims, sources, privacy obligations, and applicable law.
